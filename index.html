For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk and ganulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridte and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturerinstructions, i.e.,let spin and thicken for 20 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for for at at least two hours before serving.

  • 11/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1 lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • σ, 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon, jucied
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
